Weather & What to Wear

What to expect in Lanzarote and how to dress comfortably.

☀️ Climate

Lanzarote enjoys warm temperatures year-round. In March, you can expect daytime temperatures around 20–25°C with plenty of sunshine.

Evenings can feel cooler, especially near the coast.

🌬️ Microclimates

The island has subtle microclimates — Playa Blanca is typically sunnier and warmer, while other areas may feel windier or slightly cooler.

A light breeze is common, particularly in the evenings.

🧥 Evening Wear

We recommend bringing a light jacket, cardigan or hoodie for evenings, especially after sunset or by the sea.

Layers are the best option to stay comfortable throughout the day.

👠 Footwear

Some paths, pavements and roads can be uneven.

Stiletto heels may be tricky in certain areas, so consider wedges, block heels or flats for comfort.

Smoking

The venue is a smoke-free campus. Please use designated smoking areas only. Kindly refrain from smoking or vaping at the table during meal times.
